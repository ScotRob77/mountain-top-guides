What we do

Summer Hill Walking

We can guide you or your group to any mountain range throughout the UK. Let one of our experienced guides take you to the wildest, most secret places in Britain. With our extensive knowledge we can take you away from the crowds to more secluded places that you won't find in any guidebooks. Whether it is your first time walking in the hills, or maybe you just want to be shown more of what the UK's mountains have to offer contact us now to see how we can assist you in your next big adventure.

Winter Hill Walking

Test yourself against the wild of winter with our winter mountain guiding trips. Running from December to March this is mostly based in the Highlands of Scotland where temperatures can reach -15 and winds pick up to over 100mph. This is not for the feint hearted and a good level of fitness is required. A full kit list of what is needed can be sent on request.

Winter Climbing

If you are an experienced hill walker in winter and want to take your outdoor adventures to the next level then join us on one of our winter mountaineering courses. We will take you to some of Scotland's most famous winter climbing venues and give you the opportunity to climb on routes up to Grade III. A good level of fitness is essential. Experience using an ice axe and crampons is beneficial to get the most out of the course. This is done over 2 days.
