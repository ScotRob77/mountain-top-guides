Who we are

Hi, I'm Robbie and I've been running Mountain Top Guides for the past 10 years. I am a fully qualified British Mountain Guide and I have climbed all over the world. I have been climbing the Scottish Hills since I was 5 and bagged my first Munro at the age of 7. I have since climbed in every continent. I worked in the Alps for 10 years before moving back to Scotland 11 years ago. My greatest achievement was reaching the summit of Everest in 2015.
Hi, I'm Lexi and I've been working at Mountain Top Guides for 5 years. I have been working in the outdoor industry for many years. I was a rock climbing instructor in California for 6years before moving to Dubai to become a watersports instructor. Although it was great fun out there I wa missing home, so I returned to the UK to become a qualified Mountain guide. I passed my WMCI back in 2016 and joined Mountain Top Guides in 2017. If I'm not venturing out into the hills of the UK you will find me splashing about somewhere off the wonderful west coast of Scotland.
