Who we are

Hi, I'm David and I've been running Mountain Top Guides for the past 10 years. I am a fully qualified British Mountain Guide and I have climbed all over the world. I have been climbing the Scottish Hills since I was 5 and bagged my first Munro at the age of 7. I have since climbed in every continent. I worked in the Alps for 10 years before moving back to Scotland 11 years ago. My greatest achievement was reaching the summit of Everest in 2015.
Hi, I'm Caya and I've been working at Mountain Top Guides for 5 years. I have been working in the outdoors for many years. I was a rock climbing instructor in California for 6 years before moving to Dubai to become a watersports instructor. I gained my WMCI 6 years ago before joining Mountain Top Guides.
